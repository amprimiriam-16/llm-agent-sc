# Inventory Management - Sample 4

## Inventory Optimization and Control

### Inventory Strategy

Dynamic inventory management approach balancing cost efficiency with service excellence across the global supply chain.

### Inventory Categories

#### Raw Materials
- Average holding: 45 days
- Safety stock: 15 days for critical materials
- Total value: €2.3 billion globally
- Management: Just-in-Time with strategic buffers

#### Work-in-Process (WIP)
- Average cycle time: 7-14 days
- Real-time tracking through manufacturing
- Optimization target: 20% reduction by 2027
- Value: €800 million

#### Finished Goods
- Target inventory days: 30-35 days
- Regional distribution center stock
- Total value: €3.1 billion
- Fill rate target: >98%

#### Spare Parts and Maintenance
- Critical equipment spares maintained
- Predictive maintenance integration
- Value: €450 million
- Availability target: 99.5%

### Inventory Management Systems

#### Platform Integration
- Real-time inventory visibility across 350+ locations
- Automated reorder point calculations
- Multi-echelon inventory optimization
- Demand forecasting and planning

#### ABC Analysis

**A-Items (20% of SKUs, 80% of value)**
- Daily monitoring and optimization
- Advanced forecasting models
- Strategic safety stock levels
- Supplier collaboration programs

**B-Items (30% of SKUs, 15% of value)**
- Weekly monitoring
- Standard forecasting methods
- Moderate safety stock
- Regular supplier review

**C-Items (50% of SKUs, 5% of value)**
- Monthly monitoring
- Simple reorder rules
- Minimal safety stock
- Bulk ordering strategies

### Demand Forecasting

#### Methods Used
- Statistical forecasting (ARIMA, exponential smoothing)
- Machine learning models for complex patterns
- Collaborative planning with customers
- Market intelligence integration

#### Forecast Accuracy
- Target: 85% for A-items
- Current performance: 83%
- Monthly forecast review and adjustment
- Bias tracking and correction

### Safety Stock Optimization

#### Calculation Methodology
- Service level-based calculation
- Lead time variability consideration
- Demand variability analysis
- Cost-service trade-off optimization

#### Dynamic Adjustment
- Automated rebalancing based on:
  - Demand pattern changes
  - Supplier performance variations
  - Seasonal factors
  - Market conditions

### Warehouse Operations

#### Slotting Optimization
- High-velocity items in prime locations
- Quarterly slotting review
- ABC-based location assignment
- Pick path optimization

#### Cycle Counting
- Daily cycle counts for A-items
- Weekly for B-items
- Monthly for C-items
- Target accuracy: >99%

#### Inventory Turnover
- Overall target: 8-10 turns per year
- Finished goods: 10-12 turns
- Raw materials: 6-8 turns
- Obsolescence management: <2% of total value

- 100% barcode coverage
